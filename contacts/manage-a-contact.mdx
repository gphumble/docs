Open any contact to manage everything about your relationship with them. This page covers the most common updates you'll make from a contact's profile.

## Edit contact details

Click **Edit Contact** to update the contact's name and address. When you change an address, you can look up the county and legislative districts again so they stay accurate.

## Manage people (households)

For households, the **People** section lists everyone in the contact.

- **Add a person** — click **Add Individual** and enter their name and contact methods.
- **Edit a person** — open a person to update their name, emails, and phones, including which are **Primary** and **Opted in**.
- **Remove a person** — deleting a person is permanent and asks you to confirm first.

### Contact methods

Each person can have multiple emails and phone numbers. For each, you can mark one **Primary** of its kind and toggle **Opted in** to track communication consent. Opt-in status matters for email—contacts who aren't opted in won't receive campaigns.

## Address, county, and districts

The **Contact info** section shows the mailing address and, when available, the county and congressional/state house/state senate districts. If the address has been geocoded, you can open a map to see the district boundaries.

## Tags

Tags help you group and segment contacts. In the **Tags** section, click **Edit** to add or remove tags. (Donor status is calculated automatically and isn't something you set by hand—see [Donor status](/contacts/donor-status).)

## Notes

Use **Notes** to capture context—call summaries, preferences, relationship history. Click **Add Note** to create one. Notes appear newest-first. You can edit or delete a note you created.

## Record a gift

Click **Record gift** to log a donation—amount, date, and details. New gifts appear in the activity timeline and update the contact's giving summary and [donor status](/contacts/donor-status) automatically.

## Log a touch

Click **Log touch** to record an interaction such as a call, email, or meeting. Touches appear in the activity timeline so your whole team can see the latest engagement.

## Delete a contact

If you need to remove a contact, use **Delete contact** and confirm when prompted.

<Warning>
  A contact that has recorded gifts can't be deleted—this protects your giving history. Deletion is permanent and can't be undone.
</Warning>
