Welcome to the Advocora Help Center. Advocora is the all-in-one CRM for nonprofit and advocacy organizations—a single place to manage your supporters, track giving, run email campaigns, and collect donations.

These guides are written for the people who use Advocora every day: program staff, development teams, and administrators. Each one is task-focused, so you can find the answer to "how do I…" and get back to work.
