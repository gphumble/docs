The **Dashboard** is the first thing you see when you sign in. It's a living snapshot of your organization's giving—designed so you can tell how things are going in about ten seconds, then act on what matters most.

<Note>
  Most cards refresh on their own and update on your organization's time zone, not your browser's. If a single card can't load, the rest of the dashboard still works—just refresh.
</Note>

## The morning pulse

The large card at the top opens with a plain-language headline about your week, followed by three numbers:

- **This week** — total raised, with the change versus last week
- **Gifts this week** — how many gifts came in
- **New donors** — first-time givers this week

It re-checks itself every few minutes, so you can leave the dashboard open and watch the day unfold.

## Today's Three

Alongside the pulse, **Today's Three** suggests up to three donors worth reaching out to today, each with a short reason. Log the outreach and the row shows **Done** (you can undo it). Clear all three and you'll see an _All caught up_ banner.

<Tip>
  Today's Three fills in once you have enough giving history. A brand-new organization may see it empty at first—that's expected.
</Tip>

## Reading the rest of the page

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="KPI strip" icon="chart-simple">
    A row of key metrics—dollars, rates, and counts—each showing the trend versus the prior period. Hover the info icon on a tile for an explanation.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who's staying (retention)" icon="users-line">
    Shows what share of each year's new donors are still giving. **Open report** reveals the full cohort breakdown with a CSV download.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The river of giving" icon="chart-line">
    Monthly giving totals across three years. Use **Compare years** to overlay any years you choose.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The rhythm, monthly value & drift" icon="wave-pulse">
    The health of your recurring program: how many recurring gifts are active, their effective monthly value, and which recurring donors have drifted (no gift in 60\+ days). **Review them** opens the list to act on.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Recent contributions" icon="clock-rotate-left">
    The latest gifts, filterable by date range, designation, and account. Click any row to open the Donor Drawer.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## The Donor Drawer

Clicking a donor anywhere on the dashboard slides open the **Donor Drawer**—a quick profile without leaving the page. It shows their last gift, lifetime total, giving cadence, a short story of their history, and a suggested next step. From the footer you can:

- **Compose thank-you** — send a personal thank-you email
- **Log a touch** — record a call, meeting, or note
- **Open full profile** — jump to the complete [contact record](/contacts/manage-a-contact)
