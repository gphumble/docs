The **Integrations** page connects Advocora to an outside email marketing provider, so you can keep using a tool your team already knows while your data stays in sync.

## Available integrations

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Mailchimp" icon="mailchimp">
    Sync email list members to Mailchimp audiences and pull engagement back.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Constant Contact" icon="envelope">
    Sync members to Constant Contact and pull open/click activity back.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Connecting

Choose **Connect** on a provider and follow the prompts to authorize it. Once connected, the provider shows a **Connected** badge and a **Manage** button for its settings.

<Note>
  You can have **one** provider connected at a time. To switch, open the connected provider's management page and disconnect it first—then connect the other.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Once connected, unmatched subscribers from your provider show up in [Subscriber match](/data-management/subscriber-match) so you can tie them to contacts.
</Tip>
