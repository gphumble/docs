The **General** page (under Settings) holds your organization's core details. They appear on donation pages, receipts, and public event pages, and they set the time zone everything is measured in.

## Editing your details

Each detail sits on its own row with an **Edit** button. Choose **Edit**, change the value, and save. You can set:

| Field | Notes |
| --- | --- |
| **Display name** | Required—how your organization is shown |
| **EIN** | Formats automatically as XX-XXXXXXX |
| **Mailing address** | Street, city, state, ZIP |
| **Phone** | Formats automatically |
| **Website** | Your public site |
| **Billing contact** | Name, email, phone |
| **Timezone** | The basis for dates and "this week" everywhere |

## Your logo

Upload a logo (PNG, JPEG, WebP, or SVG, up to 2 MB) in the logo row—drag-and-drop works too. **Replace** or **Remove** it anytime. It shows on donation pages, receipts, and your public event page header.

<Warning>
  Your **time zone** drives reporting windows, scheduled sends, and dashboard metrics. Set it correctly before you rely on the numbers.
</Warning>
