## How it fits together

A **campaign** is a single send. It uses a **template** (or content you build fresh) and goes to an audience—either a **list** or a **segment**. After it sends, **analytics** show how it did.

<Note>
  Before your first send, your organization needs a verified sending domain so emails come from your own address. An administrator sets this up under [Sending domains](/settings/sending-domains).
</Note>
